Web Audio Hack Day

Q. What is this WebAudio you speak of?

A. WebAudio is a (widely supported) W3C standard for generating, analysing and playing audio in the Browser. Checkout the spec here (http://webaudio.github.io/web-audio-api/)

Q. Where can I learn more about WebAudio

A. WebAudio School is a self-help style tutorial for learning WebAudio (http://mmckegg.github.io/web-audio-school/).

Q. What shall I bring to this HackDay?

A. Please bring you down laptop, headphones and anything else you want to use for your project, like MIDI controllers, Arduino, etc.
